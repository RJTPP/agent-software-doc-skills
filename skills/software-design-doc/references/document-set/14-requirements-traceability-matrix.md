## 14. Requirements Traceability Matrix
- What to include: detailed mapping between requirements, design coverage, and open issues.
- Minimum evidence: critical requirements are traceable to concrete design artifacts.

### 14.1 Requirement to Design Mapping Table
- What to include: structured requirement-to-section/component mapping.
- Minimum evidence: requirement identifiers and owning design artifacts are explicit.

### 14.2 Coverage Status and Open Items
- What to include: covered, partial, deferred, and missing items.
- Minimum evidence: each open item has a next action or owner.
