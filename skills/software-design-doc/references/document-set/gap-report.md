# SDD Gap Report

## Scope and Inputs
- What to include: source files reviewed, assumptions made, and analysis scope.

## Missing Required Content
- What to include: absent files, headings, formal artifacts, or architecture coverage.

## Weak or Implicit Rationale
- What to include: decisions or tradeoffs that are present but under-explained.

## Traceability Gaps
- What to include: missing or weak mappings from requirements/concerns to design coverage.

## Recommended Fixes (Priority Ordered)
- What to include: concrete remediation actions in execution order.

## Coverage Summary
- What to include: overall readiness assessment, residual risks, and notable `N/A rationale` items.
