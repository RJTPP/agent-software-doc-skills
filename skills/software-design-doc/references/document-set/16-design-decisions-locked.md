## 16. Design Decisions (Locked)
- What to include: decisions intentionally locked for the implementation cycle and the constraints they create.
- Minimum evidence: locked decisions are distinguishable from open questions.

### 16.1 Architectural Decisions and Rationale
- What to include: finalized architecture decisions with rationale and downstream impact.
- Minimum evidence: each locked decision includes consequence notes.

### 16.2 Deferred Decisions
- What to include: known decisions intentionally postponed beyond the current design cut.
- Minimum evidence: deferred decisions have explicit triggers or future review points.
